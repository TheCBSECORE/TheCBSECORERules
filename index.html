🚨 THE "INSTANT BAN" ZONE

1. RESPECT WOMEN (OUR MAIN RULE) Any disrespect, harassment, or abuse toward female members is an INSTANT PERMANENT BAN. No warnings, no appeals.
2. NO SELF-PROMOTION / DM ADVERTISING Advertising YouTube, Discord servers, or other platforms results in an Instant Ban. This includes unsolicited DMs to server members.
3. NO SIMPING / FLIRTING This is for growth, not dating. Any form of simping or making members uncomfortable will result in an immediate permanent ban.
4. ALT ACCOUNTS & LOYALTY One person, one account. All alts and main will be banned. We do not tolerate "snakes"—if you have an issue, tell staff or leave.
5. THE 5-STRIKE & ACCOMPLICE POLICY 5 Warnings = Instant Ban. If you lie for or cover for a rule-breaker, you receive the exact same punishment as them.

📚 STUDY & VC ETIQUETTE

STAY PRODUCTIVE Move to Chill Convo VCs if not studying. Idlers in focus areas will be moved by staff. The study environment is sacred.
RESOURCE PROTECTION Do not steal or copy our curated resources to other servers. This is theft of community effort and will be prosecuted.
CAM ONLY VC Do not join Cam VCs without a camera. Trollers will receive a Temporary Ban.
DO NOT INTERRUPT If someone is studying or actively helping another member in VC, do not interrupt them. Any behavior that annoys or distracts those working toward the server’s prime objective is unacceptable and will result in a Mute or Ban.
RESPECT THE ENVIRONMENT The study environment is sacred. Keep noise, jokes, and off-topic chatter out of designated focus zones.
DOUBT HELPERS Use specific channels. Do not interrupt active study/help sessions in VC. Noise and jokes are prohibited in focus zones.

💬 CHAT & CONDUCT

FREEDOM OF SPEECH LIMITS Friendly arguments are fine, BUT this never applies to Women, Religion, Politics, or Racism.
NUDITY / NSFW / SPAM Nudity = 1-Day Timeout + Warning. No walls of text, emoji spam, or unnecessary @everyone tags.
HELPER ETIQUETTE Respect Helpers' time. Do not ping for "stupid reasons" or non-academic issues. They are not your personal search engine.

🎫 TICKETS & SUPPORT

REPORTING & TICKETS DM the Server Bot or Create a Ticket for issues. Provide evidence (audio/screenshot). Troll tickets result in a Warning.
STAFF DISCRETION If a Moderator tells you to change the topic, do it immediately. Their word is final.

Founded in December 2025 by NotLaksh_ and OkBye, THE CBSE CORE is a premier digital collective for Indian students. We provide a high-performance ecosystem for Class IX, X, JEE, and NEET aspirants to study, collaborate, and grow.

The server is led by NotLaksh_ and OkBye. Their vision was to create a distraction-free, resource-rich environment that levels the playing field for every Indian student, regardless of their coaching or background.

Our environment is built on Productivity. We provide curated notes, formula sheets, and a master resource database. To maintain focus, we enforce strict noise and behavior rules in our designated Study VCs.
